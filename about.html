About Us

Powering Industries, Empowering Success

Energy Corporates managed by a team of engineers is an ideal solution house for utilities of water, steam and heat. Founded in 2010; it provides sales & services in all products of Thermax Limited. The Company is an Authorized Channel Partner of Thermax Ltd a Indian Major dealing in the Utilities such as Power, Steam and Water.

Energy Corporates operates out of Nigdi, Pune a major Industrial hub in India. The proprietor Mr Mahesh Kulkarni has a wide experience in the field of Process heat. A engineer by qualification who has worked with Thermax Ltd for 21 years in India and South East Asia.
